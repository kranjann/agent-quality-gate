Information Security Policy

1. Employees must use multi-factor authentication for all corporate applications.
2. Passwords must contain at least 12 characters and should not be shared.
3. Confidential company information must only be stored in approved corporate systems.
4. USB storage devices are prohibited unless explicitly approved by the IT Security team.
5. Suspicious emails must be reported using the phishing reporting tool.
6. Software may only be installed through approved IT channels.
7. Employees must lock their computers whenever they leave their desks.
8. Security incidents must be reported within one hour of discovery.
9. Customer data must be encrypted during storage and transmission.
10. Annual security awareness training is mandatory for all employees.
