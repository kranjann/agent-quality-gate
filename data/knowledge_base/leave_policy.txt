Employee Leave Policy

1. Full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of annual leave per calendar year.
2. Annual leave must be approved by the employee's manager before it is taken.
3. A maximum of 5 unused leave days may be carried forward to the next calendar year.
4. Sick leave is granted separately from annual leave and requires medical documentation if it exceeds three consecutive working days.
5. Casual leave cannot be carried forward.
6. Employees on probation receive leave on a prorated basis.
7. Public holidays are not counted as annual leave.
8. Leave requests should be submitted at least five working days in advance whenever possible.
9. Unapproved absences may result in disciplinary action.
10. HR is responsible for maintaining employee leave balances.
