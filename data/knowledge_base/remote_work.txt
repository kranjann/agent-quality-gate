Remote Work Policy

1. Employees may work remotely for up to three days per week with manager approval.
2. Employees must be available during core business hours from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM.
3. Company-issued laptops must be used when working remotely.
4. Public Wi-Fi networks must not be used unless connected through the corporate VPN.
5. Confidential company information must not be stored on personal devices.
6. Employees are responsible for maintaining a secure working environment at home.
7. Managers may require employees to attend office meetings when business needs arise.
8. Remote work arrangements are reviewed every six months.
9. Internet connectivity issues should be reported immediately to the IT Service Desk.
10. Failure to comply with security requirements may result in suspension of remote work privileges.
