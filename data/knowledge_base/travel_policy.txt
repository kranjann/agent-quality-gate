Business Travel Policy

1. Business travel must be approved by the employee's manager before booking.
2. Economy class should be used for domestic flights.
3. Business class is permitted only for international flights exceeding eight hours.
4. Employees should use preferred corporate hotels whenever available.
5. Personal travel expenses will not be reimbursed.
6. Travel itineraries must be shared with the manager before departure.
7. Employees should purchase travel insurance for international trips.
8. Lost receipts should be reported to the Finance department immediately.
9. Emergency travel may bypass normal approval with executive authorization.
10. Employees must submit travel expense reports within 15 days after returning.
